◢ What it does

A real mixing desk for any track.

Not a vocal remover with two sliders — a full multitrack you can actually play with.

✂

6-stem separation

An AI model (Demucs) splits the song into vocals, drums, bass, guitar, piano and other — cleanly, on your GPU.

🎚

A fader per instrument

Volume, mute and solo on every channel, with a live level meter and a master — mix it exactly how you want.

♯

Transpose & time-stretch

Change the key and the tempo independently, in real time. Drop it a step, slow it to half-speed to learn a part.

↺

A–B loop & count-in

Set two points and loop a hard section until it sticks, with a one-bar count-in before playback.

●

Record along

Add a track, arm it, and play over the stems. Your take is captured in sync and sits right in the mixer.

▶

Stage mode

A full-screen, big-button player for playing live — big Prev / Play / Next, key & tempo, and a song list.

♪

Chords & synced lyrics

Chord-per-beat and time-synced lyrics scroll as the song plays — and transpose right along with the key.

⬇

Export it

Bounce your mix or every isolated stem to MP3, WAV, FLAC and more — including a click/practice split.

◢ How it runs

It all happens on your machine.

FullBand is a small local engine plus a mixer. The engine (Demucs on your GPU) does the heavy separation; the mixer runs in your browser or as a native Windows app. There's no cloud step, no upload, and no account — the song never leaves your computer.

No cloudNo uploadNo accountNo adsOpen source
01Clone the repo from GitHub — or download the ZIP (green Code ▸ Download ZIP).
02Run setup.bat — one command installs Python, the AI model, ffmpeg and the UI.
03Run run.bat — FullBand opens at localhost:8000 (or launch the desktop app).
04Paste a link, hit Separate, and start mixing.
